Leffe

Leffe (English pronunciation: /ˈlɛf/ or /ˈlɛfə/) is a beer brand owned by InBev Belgium, a subsidery of Anheuser–Busch InBev. There are several beers in the range, and they are marketed as Abbey beers. They are brewed in large quantities and are widely distributed.
